About Fakultas Teknik

Fakultas Teknik Universitas Maritim Raja Ali Aji is a Faculty of Engineering located in Pekanbaru, Riau, Indonesia. The faculty offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs in the field of engineering including Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Industrial Engineering, and Marine Engineering.
